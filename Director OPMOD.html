Office of Performance Management and Open Data

Use your human and data skills to lead a team of dedicated data public servants.

The Director of the Office of Performance Management and Open Data (OPMOD) works with city departments and agencies to understand the end users needs, improve city processes and deliver value, all in a data informed manner. If you are the type of person who falls in love with the problem as much as the solution, if you enjoy coaching others to excellence, if you are comfortable working in complex and abigious environments, well then, this job is for you!


  Working at the City of Chattanooga

The One Chattanooga Strategic Framework is the north star for our work at the city. Centered on equity, One Chattanooga consists of 7 goals and 40 initiatives to realize our vision of a city where opportunity exists for all and where prosperity is plentiful. By starting with the idea that local government should optimize for people and not the other way around, we can realize a more effective and equitable city. The One Chattanooga Values are the following:

  • Serve Others (with urgency)

  • Treat Others with Dignity and Respect

  • Be a Problem Solver

  • Do the Right Thing

City Benefits The City of Chattanooga has amazing benefits including great medical plans and a dedicated city wellness center, to name a few. The city observers 12 holidays and starts out with 27 days of paid time off for a total of 39 days off each year. There is a lot more to discover. You can learn more about city benefits here.

Innovation Delivery and Performance Department (IDP) serves as an internal support group to better the work of each city department and help achieve the One Chattanooga vision. IDP is a department of connecting the dots and is a great place for individuals who are curious and love learning. There is always something new to learn in the city when you work with departments as diverse as police, fire, public works, technology services and community development, for example.
