  The Internship

We want our internships to be mutually beneficial for the interns and the office. Interns will gain greater insights into how the city operates and expands their real world use of data to drive change.

We typically mentor through job shadowing (inviting interns to important meetings and discussions) and making ourselves accessible and open for questions and interruptions. We are not the type to sit someone down and lecture but prefer to show by doing rather than telling.

For the Performance Analytics internship, we will be working collaboratively on the projects below:

  • The Primary Project- The city's data science program Chattalytics helps departments proactively address issues before they occur. The Performance Analytics intern will assist with managing Chattalytics projects and model development. Day to day work will include coordinating with departments, data collection and preparation, model development and model testing.

  • The Secondary Project- The Office of Performance Management and Open Data often receives special requests for data analysis from departments and the mayor's office. These adhock requests are short in duration and smaller in scope. The Performance Analytics intern will assist with adhock requests as they come into the office. .
